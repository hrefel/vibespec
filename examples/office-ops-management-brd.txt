3. BUSINESS REQUIREMENTS
========================================

BR-001: Workspace Management
Priority: High
Description: The system must enable booking and management of office workspaces including desks, meeting rooms, and collaborative areas.
Business Value: Optimize space utilization and eliminate booking conflicts
Acceptance Criteria:
- Users can view available spaces in real-time
- Users can book spaces up to 30 days in advance
- System sends booking confirmations and reminders
- Administrators can set booking rules and restrictions

BR-002: Visitor Management
Priority: High
Description: The system must track and manage office visitors including registration, check-in/check-out, and badge printing.
Business Value: Enhance security and streamline visitor experience
Acceptance Criteria:
- Visitors can pre-register online
- System generates QR codes for visitor passes
- Reception staff can check visitors in/out
- Host employees receive notifications when visitors arrive

BR-003: Asset Management
Priority: Medium
Description: The system must track office assets including equipment, furniture, and IT devices.
Business Value: Prevent asset loss and optimize asset utilization
Acceptance Criteria:
- Complete inventory of all office assets
- Track asset location, condition, and assignment
- Enable equipment checkout/return workflows
- Generate asset utilization reports

BR-004: Service Request Management
Priority: High
Description: The system must allow employees to submit and track service requests for facilities, IT, and administrative support.
Business Value: Improve response times and service quality
Acceptance Criteria:
- Employees can submit requests with descriptions and attachments
- Requests are automatically routed to appropriate teams
- Real-time status tracking and notifications
- Service level agreement (SLA) monitoring

BR-005: Meeting Room Coordination
Priority: High
Description: The system must manage meeting room bookings including catering, AV equipment, and setup requirements.
Business Value: Eliminate double bookings and ensure meeting readiness
Acceptance Criteria:
- Calendar integration with Outlook/Google Calendar
- Display room availability on door tablets
- Automatic release of unused bookings after 15 minutes
- Coordinate meeting services (catering, tech support)

BR-006: Employee Directory
Priority: Medium
Description: The system must provide a searchable employee directory with contact information, location, and availability.
Business Value: Improve internal communication and collaboration
Acceptance Criteria:
- Search by name, department, role, or skills
- Display real-time office presence status
- Integration with organizational chart
- Direct messaging and calendar scheduling

BR-007: Parking Management
Priority: Medium
Description: The system must manage parking space allocation and visitor parking permits.
Business Value: Optimize parking utilization and reduce congestion
Acceptance Criteria:
- Reserve parking spots for specific dates
- Manage permanent and temporary assignments
- Issue visitor parking permits
- Track parking capacity and utilization

BR-008: Analytics and Reporting
Priority: Medium
Description: The system must provide dashboards and reports on office operations metrics.
Business Value: Enable data-driven decision making
Acceptance Criteria:
- Real-time dashboards for key metrics
- Scheduled and on-demand reports
- Export data to Excel/PDF
- Customizable report templates

BR-009: Mobile Accessibility
Priority: High
Description: The system must be accessible via mobile devices for on-the-go operations management.
Business Value: Enable flexibility and remote access
Acceptance Criteria:
- Responsive web design for all screen sizes
- Native mobile apps (iOS and Android)
- Offline capability for core functions
- Push notifications for important updates

BR-010: Integration Capabilities
Priority: High
Description: The system must integrate with existing enterprise systems including HR, calendar, and access control.
Business Value: Eliminate data silos and manual data entry
Acceptance Criteria:
- SSO authentication via Active Directory/OAuth
- Bi-directional calendar sync
- Employee data sync from HR system
- API access for third-party integrations

5. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
========================================

FR-001: User Authentication
The system shall authenticate users via single sign-on (SSO) integration with existing identity provider.

FR-002: Real-time Availability
The system shall display real-time availability of all bookable resources with updates within 5 seconds.

FR-003: Automated Notifications
The system shall send automated email and mobile notifications for bookings, reminders, and status changes.

FR-004: Conflict Detection
The system shall prevent double-booking and display warnings for potential scheduling conflicts.

FR-005: Search and Filter
The system shall provide advanced search with filters for resource type, location, capacity, and amenities.

FR-006: Recurring Bookings
The system shall support recurring bookings with daily, weekly, and custom patterns.

FR-007: Waitlist Management
The system shall allow users to join waitlists for fully booked resources.

FR-008: Cancellation Policies
The system shall enforce configurable cancellation policies with automatic penalties.

FR-009: Capacity Management
The system shall enforce capacity limits based on room size and safety regulations.

FR-010: Accessibility Features
The system shall provide accessibility options including screen reader support and high contrast modes.

8. BUSINESS PROCESS WORKFLOWS
========================================

Workflow 1: Meeting Room Booking
1. User searches for available meeting rooms by date, time, and capacity
2. System displays available options with amenities
3. User selects room and specifies meeting details
4. User adds optional services (catering, AV support)
5. System validates availability and creates booking
6. System sends confirmation to user and room calendar
7. System sends reminder 15 minutes before meeting
8. System checks for no-show after 15 minutes
9. System automatically releases room if unused

Workflow 2: Visitor Registration
1. Employee initiates visitor registration
2. Employee enters visitor details and visit purpose
3. System generates visitor pass with QR code
4. System emails visitor pass to guest
5. Visitor arrives and shows QR code at reception
6. Reception staff scans QR code to check in
7. System prints visitor badge
8. System notifies host employee
9. Reception staff checks out visitor upon departure

Workflow 3: Service Request Handling
1. Employee submits service request with description
2. System categorizes request and assigns priority
3. System routes to appropriate service team queue
4. Team member claims request and updates status
5. System sends status updates to requester
6. Team member completes work and closes request
7. System sends completion notification
8. Employee rates service quality
9. System generates service metrics
